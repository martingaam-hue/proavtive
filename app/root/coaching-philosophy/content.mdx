Every coach on the ProActiv Sports team — whether they join with a Level 4 gymnastics qualification or as a sports graduate fresh from university — completes the ProActiv Sports training course before working with children unsupervised. The course covers our progression standards, safety protocols, communication with parents, and the specific apparatus and class structures used across our three venues.

The reason is simple: a parent dropping their child off in Wan Chai, in Cyberport, or in Katong needs to know the standard of care is the same. The training course is how we make that promise real, every day, regardless of which coach is on the floor.

We invest in our coaches because the coaches are the product. Equipment, premises, schedules — these matter, but they are downstream of the people who actually run the class. The ProActiv Sports training course is the most important investment we make, every year.
