## What we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect: your name, email address, an optional phone number, an optional indication of your child&apos;s age, your message text, and the market you selected (Hong Kong or Singapore). We do not collect any other personal data through the website at this time.

## How we use it

Submitted contact-form data is used solely to respond to your enquiry. The HK or SG team member who replies to you will see the data you submitted; no other third-party will see it as part of normal operation.

## How we process it

Form submissions are sent via Resend — an email delivery service used to forward your message to our internal team inbox. Resend processes the data on our behalf and is GDPR-compliant. We do not store form submissions in any other system at this time.

## Trackers and cookies

The website does not use any third-party analytics or marketing trackers at this time. No cookies are set by ProActiv Sports beyond what Vercel (our hosting provider) requires for the site to function.

## Your rights

Under the Hong Kong Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (PDPO) and the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), you have rights including access to your personal data, correction of inaccurate data, and deletion. To exercise these rights, contact `hello@proactivsports.com` with the subject line "Data request".

## Changes

This is a draft policy. The final policy will be reviewed by a lawyer with PDPO + PDPA expertise before public launch. We&apos;ll publish the final version with a clear effective date.
