## Our story

ProActiv Sports is a children's gymnastics and sports specialist founded in Hong Kong in 2011 by Will, a graduate of Dublin City University (Sports Science and Health). The company expanded to Singapore in 2014 with the launch of Prodigy by ProActiv Sports. Today, ProActiv operates three dedicated venues across two cities: ProGym Wan Chai (15/F The Hennessy, 256 Hennessy Road), ProGym Cyberport (5,000 sq ft, opened August 2025), and Prodigy @ Katong Point (Level 3, 451 Joo Chiat Road, Singapore — home to Singapore's only MultiBall interactive wall). Programmes serve children aged 2 to 16 across gymnastics, multi-sport classes, holiday camps, birthday parties, school partnerships, and a competitive gymnastics pathway. Every coach completes the ProActiv Sports training course regardless of prior qualifications, ensuring consistent standards of safety and progression across both markets.

## Our history

- **2011 — Founded in Hong Kong.** Will starts ProActiv Sports with a single class in Wan Chai. The model is gymnastics-led from day one.
- **2014 — Expanded to Singapore.** The Prodigy by ProActiv Sports brand launches, anchored by a multi-sport approach distinct from the HK gymnastics focus.
- **2025 — ProGym Cyberport opens.** A 5,000 sq ft purpose-built facility joins ProGym Wan Chai, doubling the HK footprint.

## School partnerships

ProActiv Sports works with leading international schools across Hong Kong and Singapore — including KidsFirst, ESF, the International French School (IFS), and Singapore American School — to deliver in-school sports and gymnastics programmes. Partnership details are shared with permission.
