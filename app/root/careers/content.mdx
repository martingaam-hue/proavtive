Real children, real venues, real consequences. ProActiv Sports isn&apos;t a side hustle for the founders — it&apos;s a full operation across three dedicated facilities, two cities, and 14 years of children whose parents trust us with their afternoons. The day-to-day is busy and the work is physical, but the job ends with kids walking out smiling and parents booking the next term. If that feedback loop is what you want from a career, we&apos;d like to hear from you.
