# GEO Audit Report — Acme
**Date**: 2026-03-04
**Domain**: acme.com

## Executive Summary

Acme appears in 30% of relevant AI search queries, but is rarely cited as a source (10% citation rate). The brand is absent from key comparative queries where Linear and Jira dominate. Sentiment is positive when mentioned, but low visibility limits reach.

## Core Metrics

- **Answer Share**: 30% (brand mentioned in 3 of 10 responses)
- **Citation Rate**: 10% (brand cited in 1 of 10 responses)
- **Mention Position**: 2.3 (average position when mentioned)
- **Sentiment**: +0.67 (positive)
- **Gap Topics**: 4 topics where competitors mentioned but brand wasn't

## Detailed Findings

### Strengths

- **"What is project management software?"**: Mentioned 2nd, described as "AI-powered sprint planning tool"
- **"How to track sprint velocity"**: Mentioned 1st with positive sentiment
- **Sentiment**: All mentions were positive or neutral — no negative perception

### Gaps

- **"Linear vs Jira for engineering teams"**: Both competitors mentioned, Acme absent
- **"Best project management tool for startups"**: Linear, Jira, Asana mentioned; Acme absent
- **"Top 5 issue tracking tools"**: Jira, Linear, GitHub Issues, Shortcut, ClickUp mentioned; Acme absent
- **"Recommend a project management tool for remote teams"**: Linear and Notion mentioned; Acme absent

### Opportunities

- **Comparative content**: Create "Acme vs Linear vs Jira" comparison guide to appear in comparative queries
- **Startup positioning**: Target "best for startups" queries with case studies and pricing comparison
- **Remote team use case**: Publish content on remote team collaboration features

## Recommendations

1. **Create comparison guide**: Publish "Acme vs Linear vs Jira: 2026 Comparison" with feature matrix, pricing, and use case recommendations. This addresses the 4 gap topics where competitors dominate.

2. **Improve citation rate**: Add structured data (schema.org) to product pages and publish original research or data that AI engines can cite as authoritative sources.

3. **Target startup segment**: Create "Best Project Management Tools for Startups" guide featuring Acme prominently, with startup-specific criteria (pricing, ease of setup, scalability).

4. **Publish remote team content**: Write "How to Manage Remote Engineering Teams with Acme" with specific workflows and integrations.

5. **Track progress**: Re-run this audit monthly to measure Answer Share improvement as new content is published.
